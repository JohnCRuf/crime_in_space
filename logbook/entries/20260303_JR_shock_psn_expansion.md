### Shock Note - Project Safe Neighborhoods Expansion (June 2025)

**Date:** 2026-03-03

This note documents the June 2025 expansion of Project Safe Neighborhoods
(PSN) in Chicago as a candidate region-specific enforcement shock.

---

#### Event Definition

On **June 4, 2025**, the U.S. Attorney's Office (N.D. Illinois) announced an
expansion of PSN coverage:
1. Expanded south and west boundaries in downtown Chicago.
2. Added all CTA rail lines as designated PSN zones.

The release describes PSN as focused on reducing gun violence through federal,
state, and local coordination.

---

#### Why It Could Identify Crime Avoidance

1. Precise announcement date and policy boundary changes.
2. Spatially explicit treatment geography (expanded downtown footprint + CTA
   rail corridors).
3. Strong mechanism for perceived safety shifts near transit-linked routes.

Primary design use:
- Event study comparing flows near newly included PSN geographies vs stable
  control geographies, with route-by-time fixed effects.

Main limitation:
- Enforcement intensity may vary after designation; treatment should be scaled
  with observed federal gun-case or patrol activity where possible.
