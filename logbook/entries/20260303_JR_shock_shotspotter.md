### Shock Note - ShotSpotter Decommission (September 2024)

**Date:** 2026-03-03

This note documents Chicago's shutdown of ShotSpotter as a candidate
region-specific safety-information shock.

---

#### Event Definition

Mayor Brandon Johnson announced that Chicago would not renew its ShotSpotter
contract. Public reporting states the system was turned off at **12:01 a.m. on
September 23, 2024**.

The policy change applies primarily to South and West side coverage areas where
sensors had been installed, generating treated vs less-treated geography.

---

#### Why It Could Identify Crime Avoidance

1. Citywide policy timing is sharp (known decommission date).
2. Exposure varies spatially by prior sensor footprint.
3. It plausibly changes perceived and actual gun-incident response dynamics.

Primary design use:
- Difference-in-differences using pre-period sensor exposure intensity at beat/
  tract/edge level, with event-study windows around 2024-09-23.

Main limitation:
- Decommission coincides with other violence-reduction policies, so controls for
  concurrent interventions are required.
